Pawan Trading Company (PTCO) is one of the leading wholesale beverage distribution companies located in Nari, District Una, Himachal Pradesh. Our distribution network supplies premium beverages, energy drinks, soft drinks and packaged drinking water to retailers, wholesalers, restaurants, hotels and religious tourist locations.
